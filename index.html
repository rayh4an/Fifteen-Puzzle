How to Play

Objective: The goal of the Fifteen Puzzle is to arrange the tiles in numerical order, with the empty tile in the bottom-right corner.

  1. Game Setup
    • The puzzle consists of a grid with numbered tiles and one empty space.
    • Tiles may display both numbers and parts of an image.
  2. Moving Tiles
    • Click on a tile that is next to an empty space (above, below, left, or right).
    • The clicked tile will move into the empty space.
  3. Valid Moves
    • Only tiles directly adjacent to the empty space can be moved.
    • If a tile is not adjacent to the empty space, clicking it will have no effect.
  4. Rearranging Tiles
    • Continue moving tiles into the empty space to shift and rearrange them.
    • Work strategically to bring the tiles into numerical order.
  5. Winning the Game
    • Arrange the tiles in numerical order, left to right, top to bottom.
    • The empty space must end up in the bottom-right

About the Fifteen Puzzle

The Fifteen Puzzle is a classic sliding tile game designed to challenge your logic and strategy skills. The goal of the game is to arrange tiles in numerical order, with the empty space ending in the bottom-right corner. This interactive game blends fun with problem-solving, providing a visually appealing and engaging experience.
